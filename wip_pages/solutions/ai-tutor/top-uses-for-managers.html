Welcome to the gateway of enhanced employee development

With Ahura AI, your indispensable AI-based Learning Assistant, we aim to illuminate the transformative potential that Ahura AI holds for managers like you. Not only does it empower your team members to accelerate their learning by 3-5 times, but it also equips you with invaluable insights into their learning journeys.

By harnessing the power of artificial intelligence, Ahura AI enables you to gain a deeper understanding of how your employees are acquiring knowledge and skills, ultimately facilitating your ability to offer tailored support and align their learning goals with the broader objectives of your company.

Supervisor Cheat Sheet

STRATEGIC TRENDS

Capability experts: Click capabilities, select one, and find the growing experts.

Community trends: Scroll to trends section, see top capabilities of interest and top learners.

PERFORMANCE ALERTS

General adoption: Display learners, rank by time studied to see people learning less than 5 minutes.

Distraction flags: Rank by nudges received to spot struggling learners.

Tough capabilities: Spot capabilities with high hours and low assessment scores.

COACHING INSIGHTS

General 1:1 coaching: Explore dashboard to dive into learners' specific sessions.

Addressing flags: Follow flags to learn more about what learners need help with.
